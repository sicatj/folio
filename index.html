2022, Present

SGUP Microsoft Junior Full-stack Development CLT

Trainee, Junior Full Stack Developer
After a much slower pace as a freelancer during the pandemic, and as a new mother, I reflected on my past experiences and decided to deepen my knowledge and grow my tech repertoire. This time I would want to zoom in on modern libraries and frameworks that are not only relevant and popular but are also promising to small to medium enterprises alike, who may have been forced to rethink the way they do their business. As a grateful student of SGUP Microsoft Junior Full-stack Development CLT, I want to refine my developer skills and hopefully give back not only to my clients but also to my family by providing minimum tech education and to my future colleagues and collaborators in tech space by striving to become developer advocate.

Front-end Developer & Marketing Technologist

I also joined them under a contract following the attachment as a Marketing Technologist and Front-end Developer. I helped design and implement Shopify Ecommerce Store’s Front End for merchants and end users alike.
I still continue to do freelancing on a much smaller scale.
